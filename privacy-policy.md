# Privacy Policy for Pen Fight
**Effective date:** April 22, 2026

Pen Fight is a mobile game developed by **Backspace Games**. This Privacy Policy explains how Pen Fight handles information when you install, open, and play the app.

## Summary
Pen Fight is designed to keep your game progress data on your device. The app does not require a formal account, does not ask for your email address, and does not collect your GPS location. The app uses an internet connection for advertisements, update checks, and local multiplayer features.

## Information Stored on Your Device
Pen Fight stores gameplay and preference data locally on your device so the game can remember your progress and settings. This may include:
* Your player nickname and a locally generated player ID.
* Ink points, level, scores, rewards, or progress values.
* Selected and unlocked pens, backgrounds, or items.
* Music, sound, and screen-shake settings.

This data is stored locally by the app and is used only to provide game features, save progress, and customize gameplay.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)
This app uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. To serve these ads, Google may collect and use certain data, including:
* **Advertising Identifiers:** Such as your Android Advertising ID.
* **Device Information:** Device model, network provider, and technical identifiers.
* **Ad Interactions:** Data regarding which ads are shown and clicked.

## Information Shared During Multiplayer
When you use local multiplayer, Pen Fight may share gameplay data with other devices in the same local session. This may include:
* Your nickname and selected pen/background.
* Match state, gameplay actions, emoji selections (predefined in app), and power-up events.

This information is used only for multiplayer gameplay and stays within your local network. Pen Fight does not operate a central multiplayer account server.

## Internet and Update Checks
Pen Fight may connect to the internet to check for newer versions using a GitHub-hosted file. When the app requests this file, GitHub or network providers may receive normal technical request information (such as your IP address and device metadata).

## Information We Do Not Collect
Pen Fight does not intentionally collect or request:
* Email addresses or phone numbers.
* Precise GPS location.
* Contacts, Photos, or Microphone/Camera access.

## Data Retention and Deletion
* **Retention:** Local gameplay data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
* **Data Deletion:** You can delete all player names, nicknames, and scores at any time by **uninstalling the app** or clearing the **app's storage/data** in Android system settings.

## Children's Privacy
Pen Fight does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has entered personal information as a nickname, you can delete that local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app storage.

## Security
Pen Fight keeps saved game data on your device using the app's local storage. Multiplayer communication is intended for local sessions. You should only join multiplayer sessions with people and networks you trust.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated when Pen Fight changes its features or third-party services. The updated version will be posted at the same public URL used for the Google Play listing.
